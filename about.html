Who we are

We're Benzene Games! A gaming content creation group consisting of 6 members. We do fun challenges, and explore different games. Will six sides form to make a great team? Stick around and find out!

What we do

We make gaming content that’s fun to watch and fun to create. From challenges to game explorations to spontaneous chaos, we’re always trying something new. If it looks fun, we’re probably doing it and if we haven’t yet, we will.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: bring people together through fun, entertaining game challenges. We want to create streams that are exciting, unpredictable, and full of energy.
