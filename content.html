Game: "Ophelia"

About the Game:  Ophelia is a horror-adventure game on Roblox, set in a mysterious and eerie environment. The game focuses on atmospheric storytelling, with players navigating dark, unsettling spaces while uncovering various clues and solving puzzles. The setting is often inspired by themes of mystery, mental health, and emotional struggles, drawing a lot of inspiration from the character Ophelia in Hamlet, who is known for her tragic story. In the game, players explore different areas that have been designed to create a sense of unease, and they often have to solve riddles, avoid creepy enemies, or survive dangerous scenarios to progress. It's more of an experience where you get immersed in the story and setting, rather than a typical action or combat-focused game.

Planned Activities:  We’ll split into teams and challenge ourselves in the Ophelia horror game. Each team will race to finish the game or survive longer. The losing team will face a fun punishment! Expect plenty of laughs, tense moments, and some friendly competition as we push through the game’s terrifying challenges.
